Our Story

Since 1985, Alqady Pizza has been serving authentic Italian pizzas with a passion that comes from three generations of pizza-making tradition. What started as a small family business has grown into the city's most beloved pizzeria.

We import our ingredients directly from Italy - from the San Marzano tomatoes to the authentic Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese. Our dough is made fresh daily using a 48-hour fermentation process that creates the perfect crispy yet chewy crust.
